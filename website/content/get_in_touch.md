The StrictDoc features are driven by user feedback. Some users are active contributors to the project.

## Documentation

The full documentation is at [strictdoc.readthedocs.io](https://strictdoc.readthedocs.io/en/stable/), covering installation, configuration, SDoc format reference, and guides for all major workflows.

## GitHub

Bug reports, feature requests, and contributions all go through the [GitHub repository](https://github.com/strictdoc-project/strictdoc).

For questions and longer discussions, use [GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/strictdoc-project/strictdoc/discussions).

## Mailing list

For announcements and broader discussions: [strictdoc.groups.io](https://strictdoc.groups.io/).

## Discord

For real-time chat, join the [StrictDoc Discord channel](https://discord.gg/4BAAME9MmG).

## Email

You can also reach us at [strictdoc.project@gmail.com](mailto:strictdoc.project@gmail.com).

## Office hours

Weekly open video call, every **Tuesday at 17:00 CET**. Drop in to ask questions, share how you use StrictDoc, or follow ongoing development.
